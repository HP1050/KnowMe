About Me!


I have enjoyed reading novels of various genre since long back. However, writing one was way out of my mind. But, there are situations in life that are tough to cope with. While struggling with those phases, you find yourself and gain something that you once thought to be impossible. Beginning to write a book is the result of one such phase. Learning from the people around and different situations of life has been an inspiration for my journey as an author. I love spending time around nature that helps me cultivate new ideas for my books.


Books Authored


When Love Becomes An Obsession

This story is about a middle-class boy called Harshit, who falls madly in love with Aarohi. They both love each other silently but none of them admits. One day, Harshit ends up saying what he feels about her. Gradually, over time, their love grows intense and they start making future plans for themselves. But, Aarohi's parents are quite conservative and they break-up after few days. Now, their friendship started to deteriorate. Out of his misery and solitude, Harshit takes a step that changes everyone’s life forever. The story depicts a perfect picture of attraction, love and hatred. It shows, how far one can go in absence of true love.


Heartfelt...a deep emotion

A collection of ten poems to make you feel what real love is. When you are in love, the world around seems colorful. Everything feels magical and blissful. You are filled with delight and joy. But when that love is lost, you die from inside and you have to pretend that everything is fine on the outside. You feel like you are the unluckiest and unhappiest person on earth. You start to hate yourself and lose trust on people. These poems will make you realize how deep the feeling of love can dig its root in one’s heart and what one feels when he/she is in love and state of mind when that love is lost.


Love always finds its way. Is it?

Sameer, a boy from Udaipur is happy and fulfilled with friends and family. He is studious and friendly at the same time. He never thinks of having a girlfriend. One day, Ishika proposes to him and they fall in love with each other. They have different dreams to achieve. They are supportive to each other in hard times. They had thought of marrying each other and one day, Sameer asks Ishika to marry him. What will happen next? Will she accept him? Does love always find its way? What is your opinion on the title after reading the unexpected love story of Sameer?


Blooming Feelings

An anthology that brings together words from several authors. Each content takes you on a different journey unveiling various feelings. Watch as emotions come to life, petal by petal, in the pages of Blooming Feelings.
